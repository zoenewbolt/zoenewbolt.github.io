Background

Welcome to my web page! This page explores everything you need to know about me, Zoe Newbolt, as both a person and music lover. I am a traveler, having moved four times so far. I was born in Georgia, U.S. and then when I was ten I moved to Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia for six years. I loved my time there and it definitely gave me the travel bug. I returned back to the states at 16 years old and moved to Connecticut. When it was time for college I decided I was read to go somewhere warm again, missing Malaysia, and so I made the decision to move down to Miami and attend the University of Miami! I have loved every second of it and as a senior in college it is bitter sweet deciding where I will move to next.

Besides my experience traveling and my love for it, my other passion is music. Specifically, I am a big fan of EDM and House music. I love to go to music festivals to listen to my favourite DJ (Claptone) live. Additionally, I enjoy listening to this type of music when I go on my runs. My favourite place to run while listening to EDM in Miami is by the shore in Coconut Grove. It is a beautiful spot, especially at sunrise and I will miss it when I leave Miami.
